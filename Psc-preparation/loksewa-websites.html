About Lok Sewa Aayog in Nepal

Nepal's Public Service Commission (लोक सेवा आयोग) was established on June 15, 1951 and is the constitutional body responsible for selecting meritorious candidates for government civil service positions. Under Article 244 of the Constitution of Nepal 2072, each of the 7 provinces maintains its own Pradesh Lok Sewa Aayog (Province Public Service Commission).

This page provides direct verified links to all 8 official Lok Sewa websites and their 8 online application portals — 16 total links covering Federal and all 7 Provinces. All Lok Sewa applications are now processed entirely online. For federal positions, register at psconline1.psc.gov.np. For provincial positions, each province has its own portal.

Note: Gandaki Province uses onlineppsc.gandaki.gov.np — a different URL pattern from other provinces. Bagmati Province uses ppsconline.bagamati.gov.np. Provincial PSC accounts are separate from federal; your federal login will not work on provincial portals.
