  1. The medium of written examination shall be Nepali or English or both.

  3. For MCQs, 20% marks will be deducted for each wrong answer. No marks given or deducted for unanswered questions.
  4. No calculator is allowed in the MCQ examination.
  5. Subjective questions may include one long question, two or more parts of a single question, or short notes within the specified marks.
  6. In Paper II (subjective), separate answer booklets will be provided for each section.
