About This PSC Syllabus Collection

This page provides a comprehensive collection of official Public Service Commission (Lok Sewa Aayog) exam syllabus for various Nepal government organizations. These syllabus documents are essential for candidates preparing for competitive examinations conducted by Lok Sewa Aayog (लोक सेवा आयोग) for engineering, technical, and administrative positions in Nepal.

Organizations Covered

  • Nepal Telecom (नेपाल टेलिकम): Telecommunications engineering, electronics, IT and computer science positions
  • Nepal Oil Corporation (नेपाल आयल निगम): Petroleum, management and technical positions
  • Nepal Electricity Authority (नेपाल विद्युत प्राधिकरण): Electrical, civil and mechanical engineering positions
  • Road Department (सडक विभाग): Highway, structural and transportation engineering positions
  • Ministry of Physical Infrastructure and Transport (भौतिक पूर्वाधार तथा यातायात मन्त्रालय): Infrastructure and transport planning positions
  • Civil Aviation Authority of Nepal — CAAN (नेपाल नागरिक उड्डयन प्राधिकरण): Airport engineering and aviation positions
