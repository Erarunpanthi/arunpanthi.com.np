According to law of supply, if the price of goods increases then the demand of articles
a) increases
b) decreases
c) constant
d) all of the above

a) increases
Explanation: The law of supply states that, other things being equal, an increase in the price of a good leads to an increase in the quantity supplied. Producers are willing to supply more at higher prices to maximize profit.
