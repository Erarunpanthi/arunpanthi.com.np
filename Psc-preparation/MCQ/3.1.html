A perfect frame is a frame that
a) Is dynamically determinate
b) Has more than 2 supports
c) Is redundant
d) Is statically determinate

d) Is statically determinate
Explanation: In structural engineering, a frame or truss is an assembly of members connected at joints to form a rigid structure. A frame is classified based on whether it has the minimum number of members required to maintain its geometry under load without being over-constrained or unstable. The relationship between the number of members and joints determines the static determinacy of the structure.
