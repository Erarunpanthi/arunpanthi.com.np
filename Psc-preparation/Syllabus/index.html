About This PSC Syllabus Collection

This page provides a comprehensive collection of official Public Service Commission (Lok Sewa Aayog) exam syllabus for various Nepal government organizations. These syllabus documents are essential for candidates preparing for competitive examinations conducted by Lok Sewa Aayog for engineering, technical, and administrative positions in Nepal.

Organizations Covered

  • Nepal Telecom: Telecommunications engineering, electronics, IT and computer science positions
  • Nepal Oil Corporation: Petroleum, management and technical positions
  • Nepal Electricity Authority: Electrical, civil and mechanical engineering positions
  • Road Department: Highway, structural and transportation engineering positions
  • Ministry of Physical Infrastructure and Transport: Infrastructure and transport planning positions
  • Civil Aviation Authority of Nepal CAAN: Airport engineering and aviation positions
