Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hydrology in Civil Engineering?

Hydrology is the science dealing with the occurrence, distribution, and movement of water on and below the Earth's surface. In civil engineering, it is a core subject used for designing dams, irrigation systems, stormwater management, and flood control structures.
